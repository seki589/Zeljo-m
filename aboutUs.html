About Us

Our story

We offer you our restaurant as a family business with 20 years of experience. Carefully prepared dishes of traditional Balkan cuisine in a modern ambience have characterized our menu from the very beginning. Since 2013 we have also been delivering our meals to your front door free of charge.

We also attach great importance to family and child friendliness. The little guests can expect baby chairs, a children's table, entertainment with coloring and puzzle templates and a small gift. We look forward to welcoming you as our guest soon!

Culinary

Embark on a culinary journey through the Balkans, where enjoyment is a top priority. With us you can expect a large selection of Balkan specialties. Burek, Ćevapčići, grilled food, baklava & Co. are traditionally served and shouldn't be missing, but we will also surprise you with a large variety of other delicious dishes. A rich selection of desserts rounds off the offer and leaves little room for unfulfilled wishes.

Whether you eat at our locations, order to take away, or order online / by phone, we ensure fresh and delicious food and your satisfaction.
